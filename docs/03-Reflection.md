# Reflection

## Biggest Insight

I learned that AI is most effective when treated as a collaborative partner rather than simply a code generator.

## Biggest Challenge

Deciding which responsibilities should remain human-owned versus AI-assisted.

## What Changed

Initially I planned to delegate more implementation work.

After planning, I realized that learning-heavy tasks such as Redux architecture and business logic should remain my responsibility.

## Future Improvements

- Better prompt planning
- More iterative feedback
- Stronger evaluation of AI outputs
